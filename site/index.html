Strict PASS† is binary per case — a case passes only when every clinically decisive gate holds: critical findings preserved, laterality correct, no unsafe negation, structural integrity, and terminology compliance.

The public synthetic demo suite is provided for reproducibility, onboarding, and harness inspection‡. It is not a clinical validation set, not the hidden test set, not an oficial leaderboard suite, and not the full benchmark corpus.

Editor’s note · folio i

On publishing only the things that survive every gate.

LAIBench is built on a deliberately conservative premise: in radiology reporting, average scores can hide clinically decisive failures. A report that preserves most findings but misses a critical abnormality, swaps laterality, invents an unsupported diagnosis, or breaks the required structure is not a safe near-miss — it is a failed reporting artifact1.

The benchmark therefore separates diagnostic dimensions from the public verdict. Per-dimension scores explain2 why a case failed. Strict PASS determines whether the case survives.

§ II · Método — lendo o harness

De achados visíveis a um veredito auditável, em três movimentos.

Um protocolo travado de achado-para-laudo. A demo pública verifica o harness; a avaliação controlada pontua o modelo.
Primeiro.

O sistema recebe apenas entrada visível da tarefa.

O sistema avaliado recebe apenas entrada visível: descritor do exame, achados fornecidos, locale e contexto público permitido. Deve emitir um laudo completo sob o contrato de saída.

Ele nunca recebe gold labels, critérios ocultos, respostas de referência, prompts do judge, regras privadas de pontuação, canary tokens ou metadados do teste oculto.

entrada · exame + achados visíveis locale · demo pública pt-BR oculto · gold labels nunca enviados
second.

Five evaluators read it as five different failure detectors.

The harness checks critical-finding preservation, clinical quality, terminology, guideline applicability, and evidence fidelity. Optional adjudication layers may be used in controlled evaluation; public demo runs remain explicitly separated from oficial hosted assessment.

evaluators · CRIT · QUAL · TERM · GUIDE · RAG negation · scope + uncertainty judge · controlled / hosted only
third.

A binary verdict — every gate, or none.

Strict PASS is binary per case. Any clinically decisive gate failure — missed critical finding, swapped laterality, unsafe negation, malformed structure, unsupported normalizing language, or forbidden term — drops the case to FAIL. Per-dimension scores survive as diagnostics; they do not override the verdict.

verdict · pass / fail publish · row + suite hash + disclosure oficial · controlled evaluation only

§ IV · Integrity  —  the things behind the row

Public rows are only worth as much as their guarantees.

Locked tasks, recomputed scores, hash-pinned suites, and disclosure metadata govern what gets onto the leaderboard and what stays off.

“The method can be public while the oficial test set remains controlled. What is published must be reproducible; what protects privacy, anti-contamination, and benchmark integrity stays gated.”

01
Deterministic checks.

Same input, same score. Public demo artifacts expose enough method detail to inspect the harness without releasing the controlled corpus, hidden test set, or proprietary implementation.

02
Score recomputation.

Case verdicts, suite summaries, dimension diagnostics, comparable keys, and suite hashes are recomputed before oficial leaderboard publication.

03
Grouped leaderboards.

Runs only compete if they share suite, locale, track, scaffold, and judge. Incompatible runs are filed but nunca substitui o veredito ranked against each other.

04
Submission validation.

Missing IDs, duplicates, empty outputs, or malformed JSONL make a submission ineligible. Public artifacts show counts and sanitized reasons — nunca substitui o veredito raw case lists.

05
Grouped comparisons.

Product agents, custom agents, and open baselines nunca substitui o veredito mix in a single rank. Each row competes only against its own comparable cohort.

06
Hash integrity.

Each suite carries a cryptographic hash of its cases. Any alteration to the underlying data invalidates every prior run that depended on it.

07
Contamination controls.

Controlada suites may include leakage markers to detect benchmark contamination. A system that reproduces protected content is flagged for review.

08
Uncertainty intervals.

Official rows may include bootstrap confidence intervals when task counts support them. If uncertainty is too high, rows are reported as exploratory or tied rather than over-ranked.

§ VI · Preprint  —  2026

The paper — why templates are not the right unit of scale.

A formal and architectural contribution to radiology reporting. Not a claim of clinical readiness.

Beyond Templates: Um Modelo Composicional e Limite Inferior para a Variabilidade de Laudos Radiológicos

Natan Paraiso Ribeiro · Petrus Paraiso Ribeiro · Stephanie Alba Herrera
Francisco Akira · Raquel Moreno
Laudos.AI  ·  São Paulo, Brazil  ·  draft preprint · May 2026

This preprint argues that radiology reporting is better modeled as constrained compositional generation from report-relevant clinical state, rather than static template enumeration.

It introduces the Report Space Lower Bound and the Radiology Report Variability Index to describe why broad reporting coverage grows combinatorially — and why scalable reporting systems should represent protocol, anatomy, findings, attributes, constraints, impression logic, and rendering as separate components.

O preprint é uma contribuição formal e arquitetural. Não reivindica validação clínica, aprovação regulatória, diagnóstico autônomo ou substituição da supervisão do radiologista.
